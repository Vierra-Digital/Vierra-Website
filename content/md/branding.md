# Vierra Brand Kit

The visual identity behind Vierra — our logo, colors, gradients, and type.

## Brand Overview

Vierra is a modern marketing & sales company built on a bold, premium, tech-forward identity. The brand pairs a deep midnight canvas with vivid purple-to-violet gradients to feel innovative, confident, and high-end.

Three principles guide every application of the brand:

- **Bold** — High contrast, confident type, and saturated purple accents.
- **Premium** — Generous spacing, soft shadows, and refined gradients.
- **Modern** — Clean geometry, subtle motion, and a tech-forward feel.

## Logo & Mark

The Vierra logo is a lockup of two parts: the mark, a stylized gradient "V" drawn as an upward check, symbolizing momentum and validation, and the "Vierra" wordmark in a custom lowercase italic logotype. Whenever possible, use the full lockup.

## Logo Variants

- **Full Color** — For light backgrounds & white surfaces. (`/assets/vierra-logo.png`)
- **White / Reversed** — For dark backgrounds, photos & the footer. (`/assets/vierra-logo-panel.png`)
- **App Icon** — For favicons, avatars & app tiles. (`/icon-512.png`)

## Color Palette

### Primary
- **Royal Purple** — `#701CC0` — Primary brand color; CTAs, links, accents.

### Accents
- **Electric Violet** — `#8F42FF` — Highlights, gradient mid-tone, hover states.
- **Lavender** — `#D4A5FF` — Gradient end, soft emphasis on dark.
- **Soft Lilac** — `#C99DFF` — Eyebrows & labels on dark surfaces.

### Darks & Neutrals
- **Midnight** — `#18042A` — Primary dark surface; heroes, footer.
- **Deep Plum** — `#2E0A4F` — Dark gradient mid-tone.
- **Ink** — `#1A1033` — Headings & strong text on light.
- **Slate** — `#4B4460` — Body copy on light surfaces.

### Surfaces
- **Off-White** — `#F3F3F3` — Primary light page surface.
- **Pure White** — `#FFFFFF` — Cards & elevated surfaces.

## Gradients

Gradients are central to the Vierra look. Use them on headings, buttons, accents, and large surfaces, never on body text.

- **Brand Gradient** — `#701CC0 → #8F42FF` — Buttons, Badges, the Mark.
- **Heading Gradient** — `#8F42FF → #D4A5FF → #8F42FF` — Gradient Text.
- **Hero / Dark Gradient** — `#4F1488 → #2E0A4F → #18042A` — Hero & Footer.
- **Glow Conic** — `#701CC0 → #8F42FF → #D4A5FF → #8F42FF → #701CC0` — Animated trim on the "Free Audit Call" button.

## Typography

- **Bricolage Grotesque** — Display & Headings.
- **Inter** — Body & Interface.
- **Figtree** — Labels & Accents.

## Text Styles & Scale

- **Eyebrow** — Bricolage · 12px · uppercase · 0.35em tracking.
- **Display Heading** — Bricolage · 48–72px · bold.
- **Section Heading** — Bricolage · 20–24px · semibold.
- **Body Copy** — Inter · 15px · 1.75 line-height. The default reading style for paragraphs.
- **Caption & Meta** — Inter · 13px · muted. Supporting detail and labels.
- **Heading Gradient** — Heading gradient clipped to text only.

## Visual Motifs

- **Animated Blobs** — Soft, drifting purple gradient orbs on dark surfaces.
- **Concentric Rings** — Layered circular outlines used as a footer accent.
- **Glowing Trim** — Animated conic-gradient border on the primary CTA button.
