# Brand Logo Notes

These SVGs are third-party brand trademarks, sourced from the Simple Icons CDN
(https://simpleicons.org, CC0 icon set — but the underlying trademarks belong to
their respective owners). They are used here as representative examples of the
kinds of businesses Vierra serves.

⚠️ Displaying a company's logo can imply a client/partner relationship. Only keep
logos you can legitimately represent, and prefer your actual clients' logos (with
permission) or generic wordmarks. Swap the `BRANDS` array in
`components/FeaturesSection/BrandSphere3D.tsx` to change the set.
